Vision

As Dr.A.P.J.Abdul Kalam sir says quality Education for all students and students should go on the right path towards his/her dream.Let’s create a freedom that is for students.

Mission

As a student, each and everyone has their own dream in which some of them are struggling to fulfill their dream.As a friend we will stand by them and make their dream as a goal to achieve and showing them a good path to reach success.

Students Federation Roles and Responsibilities:

Designing Team:

For students, for easy understanding we design posters for information such as Education, Sports, etc,.

Documentation Team:

Information shared will be translated into Tamil and English as headlines and it makes the information easier for students to read. Benefits: Helps to work in places like News station, School.

Human Resources Team:

Students have a proper role to play in a federation and society.They can intervene in any matter/issues and work for their rights.

Student Council Team:

Understanding the mood of the students and helping them to know that they are on the right track and let them help what they want. Benefits: We can easily find out what others want, not just you.

Marketing Team:

Taking information to the students through the internet you can publish by knowing when the students will see and what will be published at that time.

Decision Making Team:

To make sure that we carry the right information to students R&I together will make a final decision. Benefits: This will help you make the right decision in difficult situation in your life or in the life of others.And also helps to rectify the future problems in advance.

Research & Innovation Department(R&D):

In this Students Federation, we are under the research about the information announced by Department of Education on which how the students get benefited and what is useful in that.And also we take more innovative innovations to students. Benefits: Predicting the profit that comes with creating a new business.

Secretary:

You will be collecting informations of new students and informations on existing students in a secure manner and reviewing them whether the records are correct.

President:

President will ensure that all the students in each branch are working properly.

Vice-President:

Vice president will work along with president.Also working and discussing with NGOs and other clubs and forums for the betterment.

Treasurer:

To properly review and spend funds that are available in the federation. You will learn how to spend the funds in difficult situation.

Join-Secretary:

In the absence of Secretary Join secretary will collect information and if there is anything to be added to information or correction can be made.
