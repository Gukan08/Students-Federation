Who We Are?

Students Federation is a group of students who works together as a family and stand as a senior student to take the lives of others on the right way.This group will grow high in future to get a better education not only for TamilNadu students but also for all students in India.The students in this group will rule the Students Federation without any restrictions.

What We Do?

We take the news directly to students like Education, Sports, science which is more important for daily life of a student.Also we give importance to the education of the students in rural areas and take them in a right path.This students federation runs entirely only for the students.
